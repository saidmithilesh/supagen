# IAM Organization And Billing Account Boundary

Supagen treats Organization as an IAM and tenancy concept, while Billing owns the separate Billable Account concept that represents the commercial boundary for AI credits, platform access, billing history, commercial state, and billing entitlements. Every organization has a billable account, but billing references organizations rather than redefining them. Billing owns Supagen recharge, refund, dispute, and adjustment records/actions, while payment-provider records such as Dodo customers, checkouts, payments, invoices, receipts, provider refunds, and provider disputes are external billing artifacts. This keeps IAM authorization, billing entitlement reporting, and payment-provider integration language separate while still allowing Dodo Payments to be the initial implementation provider.
